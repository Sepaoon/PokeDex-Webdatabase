💱 Price Sources & Currency Conversion

TCGPlayer (USD) — the default. Uses the Pokémon TCG API to pull live market prices in US dollars. No account needed.

Cardmarket (EUR) — the main European marketplace. Prices are in Euros. Requires free API credentials from Cardmarket.

The Open Exchange Rates App ID lets the app fetch a live USD↔EUR rate so your total value is always accurate whichever source you use. Here's how to get one free:

1

Go to openexchangerates.org/signup/free and create a free account — no credit card needed.

2

After signing in, your App ID is shown on the dashboard — a string of letters and numbers.

3

Copy it, paste it into the Open Exchange Rates App ID field below, and tap Save Keys.

4

Done. The app fetches the rate once per hour and caches it — well within the free plan's 1,000 requests/month limit. Without a key it falls back to a fixed rate of 0.92.
